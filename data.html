Resource Table

Use this table to choose the best resource for each part of learning Deadlock.

Useful Deadlock Learning Resources
Resource Type Best Use Why It Matters
Official Deadlock Website Official source Checking the real game page and current official information. It is the most reliable source because it comes directly from Valve.
The Complete Deadlock Beginner Guide Video guide Learning the basic flow of a match, movement, shops, and objectives. A spoken visual guide is easier for new players than reading a long article first.
Mobalytics Beginner Guide Written guide Reviewing beginner tips, item basics, hero learning, and map habits. Written sections are useful when a player wants to reread one topic quickly.
The Deadlock Wiki Community wiki Looking up heroes, items, mechanics, updates, map details, and strategy terms. A wiki is useful during practice because topics are organized and easy to revisit.
The Bear Pit - Deadlock Voice podcast Listening to Deadlock discussion, updates, and community opinions. Audio is useful for learning passively after watching or practicing.
Start with the official site, watch the video guide, then use the written and voice resources for review.
